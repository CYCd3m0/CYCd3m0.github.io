RSA

Parameters

$$ n = p \times q $$

$$ \phi(n) = (p-1) \times (q-1) $$

$$ \gcd (e ,\ \phi (n)) = 1 $$

$$ e \times d \equiv 1 \ (\bmod \ \phi (n)) $$

Note
選擇的 $e$ 必須與 $\phi (n)$ 互質

Encryption

$$ M^{e} \equiv C \ (\bmod \ n) $$

Decryption

$$ C^{d} \equiv M \ (\bmod \ n) $$

Correctness

$$ e \times d \equiv 1 \ (\bmod \ \phi(n)) $$

$$ e \times d = 1 + k \times \phi(n) $$

$$ M^{ed} = M^{1 + k \times \phi(n)} = M \times M^{k \times \phi(n)} = M \times (M^{\phi(n)})^{k} $$

$$ M \times (M^{\phi(n)})^{k} \equiv M \times (1)^{k} = M \ (\bmod \ n) $$

based on Euler’s theorem

Correctness for $\lambda (n)$

$$\text{prove} \hspace{1em} M^{\lambda (n)} \equiv 1 \ (\bmod \ n)$$

$$\lambda (n) = \text{lcm} (p-1,q-1)$$

$$\hspace{0.2em} = k_1 \times (p-1)$$

$$\hspace{0.2em} = k_2 \times (q-1)$$


$$M^{\lambda (n)} = M^{k_1 \times (p-1)} \equiv 1 \ (\bmod \ p)$$

$$M^{\lambda (n)} = M^{k_2 \times (q-1)} \equiv 1 \ (\bmod \ q)$$

based on Fermat’s little theorem

So by the Chinese Remainder Theorem , we must have that

$$M^{\lambda (n)} \equiv 1 \ (\bmod \ n)$$


Attacks

Pollard’s p-1

  • 當 $p-1$ 是 k-smooth ，且 k 很小，可以分解 $n$

Williams’s p+1

  • 當 $p+1$ 是 k-smooth ，且 k 很小，可以分解 $n$

Fermat’s factorization

  • 如果 $p$ 和 $q$ 相近，可以分解 $n$

Wiener’s attack

  • 如果 $d$ 很小，可以分解 $n$

Boneh and Durfee attack

  • 如果 $d$ 很小，可以分解 $n$

  • 容忍度比 Wiener 大，但速度較慢

Common factor attack

  • 有多個密文

  • $\gcd(n_{1},…,n_{k}) \not = 1$

Håstad’s broadcast attack

  • 有多個密文

  • $m_{1} = … = m_{k}$

  • $n_{1} \not = … \not = n_{k}$

  • $e_{1} = … = e_{k}$

  • $k \ge e$

Common modulus attack

  • 有兩個密文

  • $m_{1} = m_{2}$

  • $n_{1} = n_{2}$

  • $\gcd(e_{1},e_{2}) = 1$

Franklin Reiter related message attack

  • 有兩個密文

  • $m_{1} = f(m_{2})$ ，兩個明文有線性關係

  • $n_{1} = n_{2}$

  • $e_{1} = e_{2}$

Coppersmith shortpad attack

  • 有兩個密文

  • 兩個明文的差距不大，如相同明文加上不同的 padding

  • $n_{1} = n_{2}$

  • $e_{1} = e_{2}$

Coppersmith attack

  • 已知明文的高位

  • 有密文和 $(n,e)$
